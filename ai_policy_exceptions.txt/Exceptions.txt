Exceptions to AI Usage Policy

1. Research and Development Projects:
   - AI models developed for internal research purposes may be exempt from certain security protocols, but must adhere to basic ethical guidelines.

2. Third-Party AI Tools:
   - AI tools from third-party vendors must undergo an approval process and be regularly assessed for compliance with security standards.

3. Emergency Situations:
   - In case of an emergency, AI systems may be deployed in a manner that temporarily bypasses standard security measures, provided it does not compromise the overall integrity of the organization.
   
4. Non-Production AI Models:
   - AI models in development or non-production environments may be subject to less strict access control but must still meet basic ethical and security guidelines.
